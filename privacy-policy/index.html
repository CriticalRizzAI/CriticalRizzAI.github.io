Privacy Policy for Critical Rizz

Last Updated: June 8, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Critical Rizz** ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use the Critical Rizz application (the "App"). By using the App, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may collect certain information from you, including but not limited to:

  • User-Provided Content: To provide the core functionality of the App, you may upload content such as screenshots of biographies, profiles, chat conversations, and photographs. You may also provide text-based context for analysis.
  • Usage Information: We may collect information about your use of the App, including the features you use, the styles you select, and the actions you take.
  • Device and Log Information: We may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage, which may include your IP address, device type, and operating system, primarily for analytics and crash reporting.

3. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving the core functionality of the App.
  • Personalizing your experience by generating relevant content.
  • Communicating with you to provide customer support when you contact us.
  • Analyzing usage trends to improve features and user experience.
  • Protecting the security and integrity of the App.
  • Enforcing our terms and policies and complying with legal obligations.
  • Measuring app performance, analyzing crash reports, and ensuring operational stability.

4. User-Provided Content and Sensitive Data

The App requests access to sensitive data to perform its services:

  • Camera and Photo Access: To enable you to upload screenshots or pictures for analysis, we request permissions to access your device's camera and photo library. This access is solely for the purpose of allowing you to select and upload content to be processed by our AI.

5. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • Third-Party AI Service Providers: We share your User-Provided Content with third-party service providers who perform AI analysis on our behalf to generate responses. We only share the necessary data to provide the feature.
  • Legal Purposes: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to a valid legal request.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.
  • Consent: We may share your information with third parties with your explicit consent.

6. Performance Tracking

We use analytics tools within the App to improve performance and enhance your experience. These tools help us understand how users interact with the App and measure the performance of various features. The information collected is used in an aggregated, non-user-specific manner to track actions and usage patterns to help us improve the service.

7. Data Retention and Deletion Policy

We are committed to data minimization and your privacy.

  • User-Provided Content (Screenshots, Photos, Context): We **do not** permanently store the content you upload for analysis. This data is processed ephemerally. It is sent to our AI provider for immediate processing and is deleted from our systems and logs shortly after your response has been delivered to you.
  • Usage and Device Information: Anonymized and aggregated usage data may be retained for longer periods for statistical purposes and to improve the app.

Because we do not store your User-Provided Content, there is no data to delete upon request. For any other inquiries, please contact us.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
